ABOUT US

Our mission

Our Mission is to delight and nourish you, our customers, with healthy, quality and delicious food, and excellent service at a reasonable price. BOLÈ NAIJA is here to bring alive the rich taste of that Port Harcourt City Bolè and Fish (and all other edible protein), in the beautiful city of Lagos, Nigeria and its environs.

Our Values

BOLÈ NAIJA, as the name implies, is here to meet the needs of Nigerians that love to eat good and healthy meals, in the most hygienic condition and environment. We are about tradition in its truest form.
We have gone a step further by creating stimulating menu to meet everyone’s need thereby increasing the range of proteins we sell and making us your number one Bolè choice in Lagos, as the taste, prices and quality of our meals are unmatched.

Our Cuisine

Proteins such as Tilapia, Kotè, Titus, Chicken, Catfish, Croaker, Turkey, Prawn, Crabs, Snails are our specialty. These are served with your preference of Bole, roasted yams, roasted sweet potatoes and roasted sweet corns garnished the Port Harcourt way with Ugba, Utazi and Onions.

Our Services

Our team manages and covers both private events and large gatherings, to assist in planning for your birthdays, weddings, corporate events etc. Be sure to choose BOLÈ NAIJA when preparing for your events, to add a side dish of the African tradition.
